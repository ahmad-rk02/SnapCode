About Us

At SnapCode, we're all about turning good posture into a fun and interactive experience. Imagine this: playing a game where your body's movements are the key! Using advanced pose detection technology, our system analyzes your body's posture in real-time. As you play the game, your movements directly influence the gameplay, encouraging you to maintain correct posture throughout.

Our goal is simple: to make improving your posture enjoyable and accessible to everyone. Whether you're a fitness enthusiast looking for a new challenge or simply want to ease discomfort from slouching at your desk, our posture-based game offers a refreshing solution. Say goodbye to stiffness and hello to a healthier, happier you!

Join us on this exciting journey towards better posture and overall well-being!
